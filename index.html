Calorie and Steps Tracker Take control of your nutrition and activity levels with FitFlow's calorie and step counter. Easily track your calorie intake and expenditure, while staying on top of your daily step count to ensure you're meeting your fitness goals.
Progress Tracking Keep tabs on your fitness journey effortlessly with FitFlow's progress tracking feature. Monitor your workouts, track your achievements, and stay motivated as you see your progress unfold in real-time.
Personalizable Workouts Say goodbye to one-size-fits-all workout routines. With FitFlow's personalizable workouts, you can create custom exercise plans that align with your fitness goals, preferences, and schedule. Whether you're a beginner or a seasoned athlete, our app adapts to your needs, ensuring you get the most out of every workout session.
BMR and TDEE Predictor Get personalized insights into your metabolism with FitFlow's BMR (Basal Metabolic Rate) and TDEE (Total Daily Energy Expenditure) predictor. Understand how many calories your body needs to function at rest and throughout the day, helping you tailor your nutrition and exercise plans accordingly.
